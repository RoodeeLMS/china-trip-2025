⚠️ Critical Weather Days

🥶
Day 15 (Oct 28) - Huanglong
Altitude 3200-4200m. Expect 0-10°C, wind chill, possible snow. Bring thermal base layers, fleece, shell, gloves, hat. Portable oxygen recommended.
🌡️
Day 19 (Nov 1) - Dagu Glacier Summit
4860m summit! Expect -10 to 5°C, severe wind chill, ice/snow guaranteed. Full winter gear essential. Weather changes rapidly.
❄️
Day 14 (Oct 27) - Jiuzhaigou
2000-3100m. Freezing mornings (0-5°C), mild afternoons (10-18°C). Large temperature swings. Layer heavily!
🌫️
Days 7-10 - Zhangjiajie
High fog/cloud risk obscuring famous views. Upper areas much colder (5-15°C). Prepare for possible disappointment!

🚨 Most Time-Critical Days

EXTREME
Day 11 (Oct 24) - ZJJ → Chongqing → Chengdu
7+ hour travel day with HSR connection. Pre-dawn start, 90-120 min connection buffer essential. Book both trains ASAP. Carry food, keep luggage minimal.
Strategy: Book earliest ZJJ train, late afternoon Chongqing train. Plan for exhaustion.
EXTREME
Day 15 (Oct 28) - JZG → Huanglong → HSR → Chengdu
Very early checkout, 2.5hr drive, 3-4hr Huanglong visit (with altitude!), 1hr drive to HSR, late evening arrival. Zero margin for delays.
Strategy: Book reliable driver, use Huanglong cable car UP, skip optional areas, confirm HSR times.
HIGH
Day 20 (Nov 2) - Chengdu → Leshan → Emeishan
Triple segment day: HSR, Leshan Buddha visit (Sunday crowds!), HSR, transfer to Emei base. Luggage storage crucial.
Strategy: BOAT tour (not stairs), 45-60 min max at Leshan, confirm luggage storage, early start.
HIGH
Day 21 (Nov 3) - Mount Emei Summit & Return to Chengdu
Very early start for first bus to Leidongping, 2hr+ journey, cable car, summit visit, return journey, afternoon HSR to Chengdu.
Strategy: Aim for 6-7am first bus, skip mid-mountain temples, have backup plan for fog.

💡 Master Time Management Tips

1
Start Early Everywhere
Beat crowds at popular sites. First cable car = shortest queues. Pandas most active before 10am.
2
Pre-book EVERYTHING
HSR tickets, drivers, attraction slots. Don't rely on same-day booking in peak season.
3
Build in Buffer Time
90-120 min for HSR connections. 30 min extra for popular sites. Weather can cause delays.
4
Accept Trade-offs
Can't see everything. Leshan: boat OR stairs (not both). Zhangjiajie: focus on highlights.
5
Pack Light on Travel Days
Minimize luggage on multi-segment days. Use hotel luggage service when available.
6
Have Backup Plans
Fog obscuring views? Long queues? Move on. Don't waste 3 hours for disappointing experience.
