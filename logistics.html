Transportation Viability Analysis

High-Speed Rail Legs

  • Oct 17: Chongqing → Huaihua South: Excellent choice. This is the standard, most efficient route. No changes recommended.
  • Oct 24: Zhangjiajie → Chongqing → Chengdu: Viable, but high-risk. This is a very long travel day (~7+ hours on trains). Your 1.5-2 hour connection time in Chongqing is adequate if the first train is on time, but any significant delay could risk your connection.
    Alternative: Consider flying from Zhangjiajie (DYG) to Chengdu (TFU). This would be much faster and less stressful, but also more expensive.
  • Oct 26 & 28: Chengdu ↔ Jiuzhaigou Area: Excellent choice. This new HSR line is the best possible way to travel this route. Do not change.
  • Nov 2 & 3: Chengdu ↔ Leshan ↔ Emeishan: Excellent choice. This is a high-frequency intercity line and is the perfect way to visit these sights. No changes recommended.

Private Driver Legs

  • Oct 15: Wulong Day Trip: Recommended. A private car or tour is far more efficient than trying to use public transport for this day trip.
  • Oct 24, 26, 28: Key Station/Hotel Transfers: Essential. These transfers (e.g., Wulingyuan to ZJJ West, JZG Station to Hotel) are critical for the logistics to work. Booking them is a high priority.
  • Oct 30 - Nov 1: Western Sichuan Loop: Essential. This part of the trip is impossible without a private driver, and your booking with DeeTrip seems to cover the plan perfectly.
