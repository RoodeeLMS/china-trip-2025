Park Ticket Recommendations & Strategy

General Rule: For all major parks during this peak season trip, aim to book tickets online approximately **7-10 days in advance**. Many parks have daily visitor caps and will sell out. Foreign tourists can typically book via official WeChat accounts or through reputable third-party travel websites/apps.

  • Wulong Karst (Day 2): Purchase the standard entry ticket.
  • Tianmen Mountain (Day 8): Book the all-inclusive ticket for a **specific time slot**. Be sure to check the change/cancellation policy, as this is the key to swapping days with Zhangjiajie Park if the weather is bad.
  • Zhangjiajie National Park (Day 9-10): The **4-Day Park Entrance Ticket** is the best choice. It provides flexibility and covers the essential park eco-buses. Buy separate, one-way tickets for the **Bailong Elevator** (ascent) and **Tianzi Mountain Cableway** (descent) on the day you use them. This strategy does not change even if you swap days.
  • Jiuzhaigou Park (Day 14): Standard entry ticket (includes park bus). This is a high-demand park during peak autumn season; booking in advance is mandatory to guarantee entry.
  • Huanglong Park (Day 15): Book the entry ticket plus a **one-way UP cable car ticket**. The recommended route is to take the cable car up and walk down to save time and manage the high altitude.
  • Shuangqiao Valley (Day 17): Standard entry ticket (includes park bus).
  • Bipenggou Valley (Day 18): Standard entry ticket.
  • Dagu Glacier (Day 19): You will need the entry ticket plus the **mandatory round-trip cable car ticket** to reach the glacier.
  • Leshan Giant Buddha (Day 20): For your tight schedule on a weekend, the **boat tour ticket** is strongly recommended over the on-foot ticket to avoid potentially hours-long queues.
  • Mount Emei (Day 21): You will need to book three separate items: the park entry ticket, the park bus ticket (to Leidongping), and the round-trip Golden Summit cable car ticket. Booking all three in advance is essential for this logistically intense day.
  • Chengdu Panda Base (Day 22): Standard entry ticket. It is crucial to book this for the **earliest morning time slot (e.g., 7:30 AM)** to see the pandas during their most active feeding time.
