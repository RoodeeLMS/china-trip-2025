Detailed Daily Itinerary Breakdown

Chongqing & Surroundings

Days 1-4: Mountain City, Wulong Karst, Hot Pot Heaven

Day 1 (Tue, Oct 14): Arrival in Chongqing

‚ñº

Plan: Land at Chongqing Jiangbei Int'l Airport (CKG) at 16:00. Clear immigration & customs. Transfer to your pre-booked hotel. Check in, drop luggage. Depending on energy levels, perhaps a simple dinner near the hotel or a short walk.

Focus: Smooth arrival, airport transfer, hotel check-in, acclimatize.

Sightseeing Rec (Evening): If energy permits and staying centrally (e.g., Jiefangbei), explore the Jiefangbei Pedestrian Street or take a brief look at Hongyadong's lights from across the street (full exploration Day 3).

Tip: Download Didi (China's Uber) and Alipay/WeChat Pay beforehand if possible. Have hotel address ready in Chinese characters.

Day 2 (Wed, Oct 15): Wulong Karst Day Trip

‚ñº

Plan: Early start. Travel to Wulong (~3 hrs drive or ~2hr HSR + local transfer). Visit the Wulong Karst National Geology Park. Focus on the main highlights: Three Natural Bridges (Tianlong, Qinglong, Heilong - requires significant walking/stairs, involves an elevator down) and Longshuixia Fissure Gorge (a narrow gorge walk). Lunch likely near the park. Return journey to Chongqing in the late afternoon/early evening.

Focus: Natural landscapes, moderate hiking/walking.

Transport: Pre-booked car/tour strongly recommended for efficiency. If using HSR, carefully check train times and local transfer options at Wulong station.

Day 3 (Thu, Oct 16): Chongqing City Exploration / Prep

‚ñº

Plan: Explore Chongqing's unique cityscape.

Sightseeing Rec:

  • Morning: Ciqikou Old Town (historic streets, shops, snacks, allow 2-3 hours).
  • Afternoon: Eling Park for panoramic city views OR experience the Yangtze Cableway (expect queues). Explore Jiefangbei Square (city center).
  • Evening: See Hongyadong (stilted complex) spectacularly lit up (explore inside/nearby). Consider Chongqing Hot Pot for dinner (be prepared for spice!). Purchase any needed supplies for trip ahead.

Focus: Urban exploration, culture, food, trip preparation.

Hunan Province: Ancient Towns

Days 4-7: Fenghuang Ancient Town, Furong Zhen Waterfall

Day 4 (Fri, Oct 17): Travel Chongqing -> Fenghuang (FH)

‚ñº

Plan: Morning check-out from Chongqing hotel. Transfer to Chongqing West or North HSR station. Take High-Speed Rail to Huaihua South station (~3-4 hrs). Upon arrival in Huaihua, transfer via Bus or Taxi to Fenghuang Ancient Town (~1-1.5 hrs). Check into your guesthouse/hotel within or near the ancient town. Start exploring the town's atmosphere in the late afternoon/evening.

Focus: Efficient travel, transition to Hunan, first impressions of Fenghuang.

Day 5 (Sat, Oct 18): Fenghuang Full Day

‚ñº

Plan: Dedicate the full day to exploring Fenghuang. Walk along the Tuo River (both banks), cross the various bridges (Rainbow Bridge, stepping stones), wander the narrow alleys away from the main crowds, visit former residences (optional, requires entrance ticket). Consider a boat ride on the river. Experience the town's transformation as evening lights turn on.

Focus: Ancient town atmosphere, photography, leisurely exploration.

Day 6 (Sun, Oct 19): Travel FH -> Furong Zhen (FZ) & Overnight

‚ñº

Plan: Enjoy a final morning in Fenghuang. Check out. Take a bus (or pre-arranged taxi) from Fenghuang Bus Station to Furong Zhen (~1.5-2 hrs). Check into your hotel. Explore Furong Zhen, famous for the large waterfall cascading through its center. You can walk behind the waterfall curtain and explore the Tujia stilted houses.

Focus: Scenic travel, unique waterfall town.

Zhangjiajie: Avatar Mountains

Days 7-11: Tianmen Mountain, National Forest Park, Pillar Peaks

Day 7 (Mon, Oct 20): Furong Zhen -> Zhangjiajie (ZJJ / Wulingyuan)

‚ñº

Plan: Morning exploration or relaxation in Furong Zhen. Check out. Travel by bus (or private transfer) from Furong Zhen to Wulingyuan Town (~1-1.5 hrs), the main base for Zhangjiajie National Forest Park. Check into your hotel in Wulingyuan. Settle in, perhaps explore Wulingyuan town itself and prepare for park exploration.

Focus: Short travel, settling into Zhangjiajie area.

Day 8 (Tue, Oct 21): Zhangjiajie: Tianmen Mountain

‚ñº

Plan: Day trip requiring travel from Wulingyuan to Zhangjiajie City (~1 hr each way). Take the Tianmen Mountain Cableway (one of the world's longest) up. Walk the cliff-hanging paths (including optional glass skywalks). Visit Tianmen Cave ("Heaven's Door") via escalators or stairs. Descend via cable car or bus on the winding mountain road. Return to Wulingyuan hotel in the evening.

Focus: Dramatic mountain scenery, cable car experience, managing logistics between Wulingyuan and Zhangjiajie City. Start early.

Day 9 (Wed, Oct 22): Zhangjiajie: Wulingyuan Park (Upper Areas)

‚ñº

Plan: Enter Zhangjiajie National Forest Park from the Wulingyuan entrance. Use park eco-buses. Take the Bailong Elevator up to Yuanjiajie. Explore the "Avatar Hallelujah Mountains" area (Southern Sky Column, First Bridge Under Heaven). Take eco-bus to Tianzi Mountain area. Explore Tianzi Mountain (Helong Park viewpoints, Imperial Brush Peaks). Descend via Tianzi Mountain Cableway. Use eco-bus back to Wulingyuan entrance.

Focus: Iconic sandstone pillars, viewpoints, using park transport (elevator/cableway/bus). High Concern for crowds.

Day 10 (Thu, Oct 23): Zhangjiajie: Wulingyuan Park (Lower Areas)

‚ñº

Plan: Re-enter the park. Focus on lower areas. Walk along the Golden Whip Stream (peaceful valley walk, ~2-3 hours one way recommended portion). Explore the Ten-Mile Gallery (either walk or take the sightseeing monorail for views of the peaks from the valley floor). Exit park via Wulingyuan entrance.

Focus: Valley walks, different perspectives of the peaks. Moderate crowds.

Chengdu: Panda Capital & Gateway

Days 11-12, 15-16, 19-23: Culture, Food, Transit Hub

Day 12 (Sat, Oct 25): Chengdu: Free Day / Prep

‚ñº

Plan: Flexible day to explore Chengdu's central attractions and prepare for the upcoming intense legs. Since you are already in a central hotel, there is no need to transfer hotels today.

Sightseeing Rec:

  • Culture/Atmosphere: Jinli Ancient Street (lively, touristy, snacks, crafts), Kuanzhai Alleys (Wide & Narrow Alleys - renovated traditional streets, teahouses, shops), Wenshu Monastery (active Buddhist temple with lovely grounds & teahouse).

Focus: Scenic overland travel into the mountains of Western Sichuan.

Day 18 (Fri, Oct 31): Bipenggou Valley -> Dagu Glacier Area
